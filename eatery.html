Our picks for the best places to eat

Fancy a memorable dinning experience? Then look ne further we have compiled a list of the best places to eat in Ireland taking into consideration the quality of the food first and formost , the atmosphere, the custumer experience , the friendlyness and professionalism of the service staff and also the overall dinning experience

Ashford Castle

Unrivalled service, warm Irish hospitality and five-star luxury . Part of The Red Carnation Hotel Collection.Discover creative Irish cuisine inspired by the finest seasonal ingredients from the West of Ireland and produce foraged from the Ashford Estate, alongside an extensive wine cellar kept in the Castle's secret passageways. With four main dining rooms ; whether it is fine dining in the grandeur of the George V Dining Room, casual dining in the beautiful setting of Cullen s at the Cottage or the atmospheric Dungeon, or a delightful Afternoon Tea in the elegant Connaught Room you will not be disapointed .
