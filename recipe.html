Spaghetti Chilli Bolognese

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan over a medium heat. Add the onion and chilli and fry for 5-10 minutes until soft and golden. Add the beef or lamb and lardons and stir oveer a medium heat until well browned.
  2. Stir in the red pepper , tomatoes and wine. Season, bring to the boil , then simmer over a low heat for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile , cook the spaghetti according to the pack instructions then drain. it is very important not to overcook the pasta!
  4. Just before serving , stir in the grated cheese , basil and the sauce into the spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion finelly chopped
  • 1/2 large red chilli deseeded and finelly sliced
  • 450g (1lb) minced beef or lamb
  • 125g (4oz) smoked bacon lardon
  • 3 roasted red peppers drained and finelly chopped
  • 400g chopped tomato
  • 125ml (4fl oz) red wine
  • 300g (11oz) spaghetti
  • 25g (1oz) freshly grated parmesan or gruere cheese
  • 2 tbsp of freshly chopped basil
  • salt and ground black pepper

T-bone Steak with chilli and garlic rub

Method

  1. Place all the chilli and garlic rub ingredients in a pestel and mortar and crush to a rough paste. Alternatively, process in a food processor or blender to achieve the desired consistency.
  2. Rub the chilli and garlic mixture all over the steaks.
  3. Preheat a griddle pan over a medium-high heat and cook the steaks for 5 minutes on each side for medium-rare , or until cooked to your liking. Cook the steaks in batches if necessary. Set aside to rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Ingredients

  • 4 T-bone steaks , 350 g/12oz each
  • chilli and garlic rub
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 2 tsp pepper
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes

Roated tomato Bulgar salad

Method

  1. Put the bulgar wheat in a bowl and add boiloing water to cover by 1cm (1/2 in). Leave to soak for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven to 220 celcius ( 200 celcius for fan oven ) mark 7. Put the tomatoes in a small roasting tin , drizzle with half the oil and add half the mint. Season with salt and pepper and roast for 10-15 minutes until beginning to soften.
  3. Put the remaining oil and the vinegar into a large bowl. Add the warm pan juices from the roasted tomatoes and the soaked bulgar wheat.
  4. Stir in the remaining chopped herbs and the spring onions and check the seasoning .( You may need a little more vinegar depending on the sweetness of the tomatoes.)
  5. Carefully toss in the tomatoes and served garnished with basil sprigs

Ingredients

  • 175g (6oz) bulgar wheat
  • 700g (1lb 9oz) cherry tomatoes or baby plum tomatoes
  • 8 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • a handfull each of mint and basil , chopped , plus fresh basil sprigs to garnish
  • 3-4 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 bunch of spring onions , sliced
  • salt and ground black pepper
